Our Mission

SafeMarket's goal is to provide safer access to the things people already buy. Academics agree on what most of us already know, online markets improve product quality and save lives. We're working on a platform that rewards trustworthy merchants and creates an economy of independent product regulators

SubMarket Design

Rather than creating a single monolothic market, SafeMarket is a platform that anyone can open up their own "submarket" and start earning comission. You can think of submarkets like subreddits, small communities dedicated to an individual product. Submarket admins are in charge of regulating merchants and settling disputes. The goal is to have multiple submarkets competing to be known as the best regulators.

1st Class Anonymity

Anonymity is the central pillar of SafeMarket. Every interaction on the market is no more traceable than a bitcoin transaction. The software handles encryption so you don't have to worry about PGP keys any more.

Censorship Resistant

All data is stored directly on the blockchain. There are no servers to take down or hack. As long as you run a node, you have a full copy of the marketplace.

FAQ

How does SafeMarket differ from OpenBazaar?

OpenBazaar has zero protections of anonymity and your IP address is directly linked with all your interactions inside the network. OpenBazaar can not and will not be integrated with Tor, i2p, or any other anonymization technology that currently exists. It is also our opnion that router-based systems (such that OpenBazaar uses) are fundamentally weaker than blockchain-based systems.
